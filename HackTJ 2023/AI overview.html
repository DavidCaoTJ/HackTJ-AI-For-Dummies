The Game
Othello: Summary
  1. Each player takes 32 discs and chooses one colour to use throughout the game. Black places two black discs and White places two white discs as shown below


  2. A move consists of "outflanking" your opponent's disc(s), then flipping the outflanked disc(s)to your colour. To outflank means to place a disc on the board so that your opponent's row (or rows) of disc(s) is bordered at each end by a disc of your colour. (A "row" may be made up of one or more discs).
